# Prevention Strategy

## Monitoring

- Add alerts for connection pool utilization > 80%
- Monitor latency and error rate continuously

---

## Capacity Planning

- Perform regular load testing (3–5x normal traffic)
- Review system capacity periodically

---

## Communication

- Coordinate between teams before traffic spikes (e.g., marketing campaigns)
- Ensure engineering is aware of expected load increases

---

## Automation

- Implement auto-scaling for connection pools
- Automate alerting and response mechanisms

---

## Best Practices

- Monitor all critical resource pools (not just CPU and memory)
- Use RED and USE methods during incidents
- Maintain up-to-date runbooks for troubleshooting
